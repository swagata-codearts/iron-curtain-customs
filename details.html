Returns

Unless stated otherwise in the product description, the product you receive from IRON CURTAIN CUSTOMS will be in NEW condition from the manufacturer. We do not sell open box or customer returned product in our web store.IRON CURTAIN CUSTOMS is proud to extend a 30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee on every order from the day the order is delivered. You may return NEW, UNOPENED merchandise within 30 days of delivery for a full refund. Merchandise that has been installed, modified, damaged, or removed from the manufacturer’s original packaging is not eligible to be returned for a refund. In some cases, open box returns may be approved for store credit. Customers are encouraged to verify product compatibility and consult with installation guides before attempting any product installation.Professional Gunsmithing is not a requirement, however it is highly encouraged for certain products in our web store* Defective merchandise under Manufacturer Warranty is subject to the Warranty Terms and Conditions as declared in the product description and/or installation instructions. We will gladly help direct you to our vendors’ customer service if this applies.You should expect to receive your refund within four weeks of giving your package to the return shipper, however, in many cases you will receive a refund more quickly. This time period includes the transit time for us to receive your return from the shipper (5 to 10 business days), the time it takes us to process your return once we receive it (3 to 5 business days), and the time it takes your bank to settle and post funds to your account (5 to 10 business days).If you need to return an item, please CONTACT US with your order number and details about the product you would like to return. We will respond quickly with instructions for how to return items from your order.

CREDIT CARD DATA

We do not store credit card information. All sensitive data is replaced with a surrogate value called a token. In a nutshell, all cardholder data is replaced with a placeholder (or token), rendering your card useless to criminals. This method removes all confidential data from our internal network. Once a credit card has been entered, we have no way of seeing the data from your card again – even our accountants cannot see your card number. Your real card data is stored at a highly secure, offsite location. IRON CURTAIN CUSTOMS complies with all PCI (Payment Card Industry) requirements.

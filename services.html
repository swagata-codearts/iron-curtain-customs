Saiga Conversions

  • Rifle Conversions

  • $399

Convert your Saiga rifle back to what is was originally intended to be. This service includes welding up the holes that shouldn't be there, adding a bullet guide, riveting in a new trigger guard and fitting the magazine catch, and installing preban parts to the barrel.

  • Shotgun Conversions

  • $399

Saiga 12 conversion is similar to the rifle with welding up the unnecessary holes, riveting in a selector stop, and riveting in a new trigger guard. This also includes a gas block alignment, gas service, guaranteed to cycle 1250 fps ammuntion. Polish bolt and bolt carrier surfaces for smooth operation.

Our hourly rate is $100/Hr. Projects that require multiple forms of work are eligible for a discount at our discretion. Some services are not listed on here either by accident or by design.

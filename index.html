About Us

Full service gunsmithing located in Houston, Texas.

Iron Curtain Customs is a full service gunsmithing shop that specializes in all things AK. Whatever the country of origin, whatever the model, whatever the caliber or gauge, we’ve got you covered. Iron Curtain Customs can handle your parts kit build, Saiga rifle and shotgun conversions, minor to major modifications, and just about anything you can come up with we can help you with. Although we specialize in AK’s, we do not discriminate. We also hold a strong affinity for HK, AR, Galil, RPD, PKM, etc. We are a full custom shop, an 07 Manufacturer, and can make just about anything you can think up a reality.

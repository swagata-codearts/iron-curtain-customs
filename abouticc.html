About Icc

Home / About ICC

Iron Curtain Customs is owned and operated by gun enthusiasts. Building guns is what we love to do, and it shows in the finished product. We are not a retail outlet selling firearms right out of the box. Just isn’t the Iron Curtain Customs way of life. Often times we look at a completed project and say….”I want one!!” So we share the same enthusiasm as you when it comes to having a firearm you can be proud to own, love to brag on, and share with your buddies. After nearly 20 years in the industry there are thousands of guns out there that started with humble beginnings in our shop. We will give you the best advice and let you make your own decision as to the direction of your project. Ultimately you, the end user is what matters the most. We challenge ourselves to make firearms that in some form or another haven’t been done before. Whether it’s a small modification or a major mashup…we completely accept the challenge. All of our work comes with a lifetime warranty. We completely stand behind everything we build.
